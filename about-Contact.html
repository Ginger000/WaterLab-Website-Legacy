Water Lab seeks opportunities to work with communities, residents, environmental organizations, water regulators, city agencies, academics, the professional sector, potential students - or anyone else with ideas for collaboration. We aim to provide a platform for discussion and exchange, a creative laboratory for ideas, and a space for learning.
