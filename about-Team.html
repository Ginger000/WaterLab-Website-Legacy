LAB MEMBERS

Mary Pat McGuire , Water Lab’s Director, is an Associate Professor of Landscape Architecture at the University of Illinois - Urbana Champaign and a Licensed Landscape Architect.

Mary Pat’s passion is water. She thinks about landscapes through water--where it lives and how it moves and interacts with the land. She lived on and off a sailboat for several years, exploring the margins of coasts, observing how water interacts along shorelines and fell in love with the changing state of this element that defines our planet.

More

After ten years of professional practice on landscape design projects and large-scale stormwater planning, Mary Pat started teaching students how to redesign and retrofit cities against flooding and run-off. She created and directs Water Lab to develop better design research for water in urban environments, and to work alongside communities to create better places to live with water.

Mary Pat’s research has been supported by Wright-Ingraham Institute, University of Illinois Research Board, IL/IN Sea Grant (NOAA), US-EPA, and the Brent & Jean Wadsworth Endowment. She has twice served as Research Fellow with the Landscape Architecture Foundation, and was an inaugural Design Research Fellow in the College of Fine & Applied Arts (FAA) at the University of Illinois. McGuire has a BA in International Political Theory from William & Mary, a Master in Landscape Architecture from the University of Virginia, and a Certificate in Leadership in Sustainability Management from the University of Chicago. She previously practiced for Peter Walker & Partners (Berkeley, CA) and Conservation Design Forum (Chicago). Mary Pat is co-editor with Jessica Henson (Partner, OLIN) of Fresh Water: Design Research for Inland Water Territories , available at Amazon
